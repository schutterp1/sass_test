Welcome to Lucky Tree Jewelry

Lucky Tree earrings are a unique find. If you’re the first of your friends to visit … consider yourself lucky!

Each pair of bright, uncommon handmade earrings comes dangling from a Lucky Tree card, creating an ornamental presentation for a one of a kind gift. Lucky Tree jewelry is for the feminine, fresh, and flirty female. The prices are influenced by Claire's strife to find affordable, stylish, unique accessories while in graduate school.

Most earrings are made for you on order. Very soon, the website will have the functionality to purchase through PayPal. In the meantime, feel free to email us and we'll work something out!
